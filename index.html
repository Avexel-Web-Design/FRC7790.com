REEFSCAPE

In REEFSCAPE presented by HAAS, alliances of up to three teams each compete to restore coral reef ecosystems by scoring coral and algae into designated areas. The game features additional points awarded during the endgame for parking under the Barge or climbing onto the Cages.

Match Format

15s autonomous, 2:15 driver control, with 20s endgame for barge ascent

Game Elements

Score coral on reefs, harvest algae, and climb onto a cage for points

Ranking Points

Earn up to 6 RPs per match through autonomous, coral, coopertition, and barge tasks

Coopertition®

Work together to deliver algae and reduce coral RP requirements
